Overview

Our business is investing on behalf of our clients — from large institutions to parents and grandparents, teachers, nurses, doctors and people from all walks of life who entrust their savings to us.

We work only for our clients & distributors. Our promise is to give them insight into what to do with their money, providing products and services that can help them build a better financial future.

OUR HISTORY

Beginning with its founding in 1968, Navinchandra Group's story over a golden jubilee is about putting clients first, performance, and remarkable collaboration.

Something Different : 1968

Founded by a man who believed they could build a better financial management firm. Dedicated to clear thinking and data-driven investing, with a passion for managing risk.

Seeds of Diversification : 1993

Mihir Shah entered the business, taking charge of Marketing and Customer Service. In 2001, he expanded the portfolio into Insurance.

"He became a 12-time MDRT as well as COT. With 18 years of experience, he set the bar extremely high in customer service."

Scale & Expansion : 2001

Navinchandra Securities Pvt. Ltd had developed strong fixed-income, equity, and advisory businesses, greatly expanding the firm's scale.
