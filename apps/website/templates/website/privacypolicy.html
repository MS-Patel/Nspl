Privacy Policy

Welcome to Buybestfin.com, a platform owned and operated by Navinchandra Securities Pvt. Ltd. (ARN 147231). Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Buybestfin, we may collect the following information:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Full Name, Date of Birth, and Contact Details (Phone Number, Email Address, Residential Address)
  • PAN Number, Aadhaar Number, and other identification details required for KYC
  • Bank Account Details for transactions and withdrawals
  • Investment Details and Preferences

1.2 Non-Personal Information

  • Device Information, IP Address, and Browser Type
  • Website Usage Statistics and Interaction Data
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies (see Section 6)

2. How We Use Your Information

We collect and use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage your investment accounts
  • To comply with regulatory and KYC requirements
  • To send investment updates, transaction confirmations, and account statements
  • To personalize your experience and recommend financial products
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized transactions
  • To improve and optimize our website and services

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Regulatory Authorities & Government Bodies: To comply with legal obligations (e.g., SEBI, AMFI, RBI, IRDAI)
  • Third-Party Service Providers: For payment processing, KYC verification, and data analytics
  • Mutual Fund Companies & Registrars: For processing transactions and portfolio management
  • Professional Advisors: Auditors, legal consultants, or compliance officers as required by law

We ensure that these third parties adhere to strict confidentiality agreements and data protection norms.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures such as:

  • 256-bit encryption for data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with multi-layer authentication
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Restricted employee access to personal data

While we strive to protect your personal data, no transmission over the internet is completely secure. Users are encouraged to adopt strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

5. User Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, modify, or delete your personal data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (subject to regulatory compliance)

For any requests regarding your data, please contact contact@navinchandrasecurities.com .

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Buybestfin uses cookies to enhance user experience. You may control cookie settings through your browser, but disabling cookies may impact website functionality.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal data.

8. Retention & Deletion of Data

We retain your personal information for as long as required by law or for fulfilling the intended purpose. Upon request, we will delete your data unless retention is necessary for legal compliance.
